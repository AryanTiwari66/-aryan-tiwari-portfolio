Our Story

Born in a small kitchen,
made for everyone

Co&Co started in 2019 in a tiny Hyderabad kitchen, where our founder Pooja Sharma spent every weekend perfecting the art of the perfect cookie. What began as gifts for friends quickly became a city-wide obsession.

Today, every Co&Co cookie is still hand-portioned, hand-pressed, and baked fresh each morning. No preservatives, no shortcuts — just real ingredients and care you can taste.
